Mukono,Uganda

Mukono Town is located in Mukono District, in the Central Region of Uganda. It's about 21 kilometers (13 miles) east of Kampala, Uganda's capital city. Mukono has several islands, including: Koome Island, Damba Island, Nsazi Island, and Ngamba Island Chimpanzee Sanctuary. The town is home to the main campus of Uganda Christian University, a well-respected institution.
